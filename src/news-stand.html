Flexible Financing

We now offer a few options when it comes to financing. You may learn more about each by clicking on the logo below.

IV Sedation

We now offer a sedation option that allows even the most anxious patients to undergo treatment. With this level of sedation no intubation is necessary, while allowing the breathing mechanisms to function on their own. The patient will remember very little of treatment after the medications wear off.

State, Medicaid, DSHS carriers

We have the ability to do full mouth extractions and dentures under the state carriers. The surgery appointment is usually scheduled within 2-4 weeks of the initial appointment date. After a few weeks of healing our friends at Evergreen Denture Center will take over the denture fabrication steps

State insurances will also cover complete wisdom tooth extractions for patients age 15-20 with IV sedation included if necessary

Charitable Dentistry

Giving back to the community is one of our favorite things to do. We participate in the HALO Dental Network in order to help the individuals in dire need. You can find some of the charity work documented in the Case Presentations section. Please refer to the link below to find out more about the HALO Dental Network

Extended Appointment Deposits

When booking extended appointments we will require a deposit to secure your spot. The deposit is refunded if the appointment is cancelled at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations within 24 hours prior to the appointment time will not be refunded.

Giving to the Community

At Pacific Park Family Dental we make it our mission to donate 20% of net proceeds to local charities, ministries and people in need
