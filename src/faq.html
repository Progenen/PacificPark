Will I get a same-day appointment for an emergency visit?

We try our best to provide same day emergency treatment, however treatment on the same day is not guaranteed. Usually any type of tooth removal happens on the same day. Root canals are rescheduled for a later date however the patients are prescribed antibiotics and pain medication.

How quickly will I get my prescription at the pharmacy?

We recommend waiting at least 2 hours before picking up medications at the pharmacy. Sometimes pharmacies are able to fill a prescription faster, but the rule of thumb is to allow some time to have the prescriptions to be received and filled by the pharmacy.
